struct fantom::cells::HyperCell< size_t >

A D-Dimensional tensor product cell with D-linear interpolation.

A HyperCell is a synonym for a D-Dimensional cell with D-Linear interpolation. It is used for automatic mapping of structured grids with line, quad, hexahedron, etc. cells to the basic and named cell types. Thus, a HyperCell<1>::type is a LinearLine etc.
